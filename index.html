Simon Says

Objective:

Repeat the sequence of colors shown by the game. With each level, the sequence gets longer. How far can you go?

Rules:

  • Press Play or any keyboard key to start.
  • Watch carefully — one or more buttons will flash in a specific order.
  • Repeat the exact order by clicking the buttons.
  • Get it right? The game adds a new color to the sequence.
  • Make a mistake? Game over. Your final score is shown.
  • Try to beat your highest score, which is saved automatically!

Tip:

  • Focus and memorize the sequence — it speeds up as you go.
